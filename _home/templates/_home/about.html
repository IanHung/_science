About

Underscore Science is a place targeted at the amateur scientist. It is a place where each is given their own lab books to record their work on projects that they drive. It is a place where they publish articles not just reporting the results of their successess, but stories recounting their missteps, stumbles, and failures; their moments of inspiration and perseverance; and their road to triumph. It is a place where community and collaboration is cherished. It is a place we hope you can call home.
